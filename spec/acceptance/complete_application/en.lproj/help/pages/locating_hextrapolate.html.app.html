Finding out where Hextrapolate is installed

Hextrapolate is installed by “drag and drop”. The most common install location is the Applications folder. If you wish to uninstall Hextrapolate but cannot remember where you installed it you can use Spotlight to find out its location.

  1. Click the Spotlight icon in the far right of the menu bar.
  2. In the search field that appears type “Hextrapolate”
  3. When Hextrapolate appears under the search results in the “Applications” group, hover the mouse over its name and a tool-tip will be displayed showing its installed location on your disk.
